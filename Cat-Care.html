How to Properly Care for Your Cat

1. Proper Diet & Hydration

  • Cats are obligate carnivores, meaning they need meat in their diet.
  • Ensure fresh water is always available.
  • Avoid toxic foods like onions, garlic, and chocolate.

Cat Nutrition Guide: ASPCA

2. Litter Box & Grooming

  • Scoop the litter box daily and change litter weekly.
  • Brush your cat regularly to prevent hairballs.
  • Provide scratching posts to protect furniture.

Litter Box Training: Humane Society
