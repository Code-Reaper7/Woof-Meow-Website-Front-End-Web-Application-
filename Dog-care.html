How to Properly Care for Your Dog

1. Nutrition & Feeding

  • Provide a balanced diet with high-quality dog food.
  • Fresh water should be available at all times.
  • Avoid toxic foods like chocolate, grapes, and onions.

More on Dog Nutrition: American Kennel Club

2. Exercise & Training

  • Daily walks and playtime are essential.
  • Basic training commands: Sit, Stay, Come, Heel, Leave it.
  • Socialize your dog with people and other pets early on.

Dog Training Tips: American Kennel Club

3. Health & Grooming

  • Regular vet checkups and vaccinations.
  • Grooming: Brushing, bathing, nail trimming.
  • Watch for signs of illness (e.g., lethargy, vomiting).

Grooming Your Dog: ASPCA
